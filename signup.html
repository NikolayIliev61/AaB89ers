Football is for everyone, regardless of physical shape, gender, height and weight. American football is about cooperation and tactics, this is a group sport where all teammates depend on each other, to achieve the best possible result. There are no requirements for joining the club, you don’t even need to know the rules, our skilled coaches will teach you everything you need to know to get you started. Regardless of your sports backgrounds, we will find the best position for you.
